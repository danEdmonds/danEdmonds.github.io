Why College Image?

It’s been said a picture is worth a thousand words.

But Forrester Research estimates one minute of video is worth 1.8 million words.

Video today is a major component of any college’s toolkit, as students, parents, alumni, and donors prefer the excitement, inclusiveness, and motivation that comes with an expertly-created visual production.

The days of the standard college viewbook are over.

A College Image video allows your institution to:

  • Showcase your campus and its many visual features
  • Explain in compelling form the benefits of your institution
  • See and hear testimonials from students, professors, and campus leaders
  • Present an wealth of information in a way that’s fun and easy to watch
  • Make a lasting impression on the audiences you most desire

A single College Image video can be multipurposed in a myriad of ways, across a multitude of social media sites, websites, in countless digital forms, on television, in movie theaters, and more.

Your school’s video is easily distributed worldwide to potential students or the education professionals assisting them with academic plans. It can be emailed directly to the inboxes of possible donors or event audiences.

Studies consistently show amazing consumer reaction to well-crafted higher education promotional videos as compared to other forms of advertising.

Today, a remarkable 80% of web users recall watching a video they viewed in the past 30 days, according to the

Online Publishers Association.

And of those people who watch your video, the following is true:

  • 26% look for more information about the subject
  • 22% visit the website referred to in the video
  • 15% make an appointment with the institution profiled
  • 12% are sold on the product

Your college deserves to make a lasting impression.

Our job is to help you make it.
