Why CI

It’s been said a picture is worth a thousand words.

But Forrester Research estimates one minute of video is worth 1.8 million words.

Video today is a major component of any progressive college’s recruitment toolkit, as students, parents, alumni, and donors prefer the excitement, comprehensiveness, and inspiration that comes with an expertly-created visual production.

The days of the standard college viewbook are over.

A College Image video today allows your institution to:

  • Showcase your campus and its many visual features
  • Explain in compelling fashion the benefits of your institution
  • See and hear testimonials from students, professors, and campus leaders
  • Present an abundance of information in a way that’s fun and easy to watch
  • Make a lasting impression on the audiences you most desire

A single College Image video can be multipurposed in a myriad of ways, from YouTube, Facebook, Twitter, Tumblr, Pinterest, social media sites, your institution’s websites, and in countless more digital forms.

Your video is easily distributed worldwide to potential students or education professionals assisting others with future academic plans. It can be emailed directly to the inboxes of possible donors.

Studies consistently show amazing consumer reaction to well-crafted higher education promotional videos as compared to other forms of advertising.

Today, a remarkable 80% of web users recall watching a video on a website they visited in the past 30 days, according to the Online Publishers Association.

And of those people who watch your video:

  • 26% look for more information about the subject
  • 22% visit the website referred to in the video
  • 15% make an appointment with the institution profiled
  • 12% purchase the product featured

Your college deserves to make a lasting impression.
