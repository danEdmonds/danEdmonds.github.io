Real Estate Law

Arnett Law Serves Morgantown and North Central West Virginia

Buying or selling a home is always exciting, but it’s also a large undertaking and often one of the most important financial transactions of our lives.

Because of the many legal and fiscal issues involved in real estate transactions, an experienced real estate attorney can provide insight, guidance, and peace of mind from start to finish.

Jeffrey L. Arnett is a seasoned, respected real estate attorney who has facilitated thousands of transactions over many years. Arnett Law Offices are available for personal consultation at your convenience.

Protect Your Investment

Whether you’re buying a first home, trading up, or downsizing, Arnett Law Offices can provide the direction and comprehensive process management to make your real estate venture an effortless and enjoyable one.

If you’re a buyer, Arnett Law Offices can:

  • Explain fully the terms of your purchase contract
  • Protect you from hidden liabilities
  • Review all title, mortgage, and tax documents
  • Register legal documents regarding to your transaction
  • Accompany you to your closing
  • Provide you with valid ownership documentation

If you’re a seller, Arnett Law Offices can:

  • Protect your interests by safeguarding purchase and sale agreement terms
  • Manage any title issues, etc.
  • Provide representation during negotiations and attend your closing

Arnett Law Offices is fully equipped to assist you with an array of property-related legal concerns. Contact us now to learn more about the real estate law services available to you.
