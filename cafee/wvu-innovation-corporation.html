WVU Innovation Corporation

CAFEE is proud to be part of the West Virginia University Innovation Corporation (WVUIC), a business/academic hybrid that provides an agile, business-focused organization.

Through WVUIC, CAFEE is able to do the following:

  • Leverage full depth and breadth of colleges to access new markets (classified and proprietary) for additional R&D revenue growth
  • Advance an innovative, customer-focused research ecosystem and entrepreneurial culture within the WVU research community that provides growth, opportunity, and financial benefits to all stakeholders, including the State of West Virginia
  • Rebalance its portfolio to grow R&D
  • Focus on providing complete back office business operations, business development, and R&D support, while freeing up R&D resources to solely focus on delivering world class research
