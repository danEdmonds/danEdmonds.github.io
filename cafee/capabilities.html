Core Capabilities and Services

Engine and Vehicle Certification

  • Heavy-duty engine emission testing via 40 CFR Part 1065
  • Light-duty vehicle emissions testing via 40 CFR Part 1065 and 1066
  • Small-engine certification ◊ Deterioration factor determination
  • Aftermarket component registration
  • Chassis testing of heavy-duty vehicles up to 80,000 pounds, light duty vehicles up to 14,000 pounds
  • Conformity of Production – Manufacturer Self-Testing

Fuels and Fuel Additive Testing

  • Evaluation of emissions and fuel-economy performance for alternative fuels and fuel additives
  • Full certification testing for aftermarket fuel conversions including CNG, LNG, Propane Autogas, DME, and others. Test-Cycle Development
  • On-road and laboratory performance evaluation
  • Road-to-laboratory
  • Custom cycle development

On-road/Real Driving Emissions (RDE) Testing

  • On-road evaluation of emissions performance for both light and heavy-duty vehicles
  • Real-world data collection and duty-cycle generation for application-specific results
  • OB3D-on-board diagnostics, development, and demonstration

Locomotive/Prime Mover/Marine (Large Bore) Testing

  • Certification-grade mobile platform for testing locomotives/vessel/ engine platforms in the field
  • Deterioration factor determination
  • Locomotive test cell fully compliant with 40 CRR Parts 92 and 1033

Aftertreatment Development and Testing

  • Wide range of engine and chassis dynamometer services available to assist in developing engines and retrofit products
  • Verification and certification

Advanced Research and Design

  • Advanced optical and endoscopic combustion research
