Core Capabilities and Services

For nearly 30 years, WVU’s Center for Alternative Fuels, Engines, and Emission has been a worldwide leader in the measurement and control of heavy-duty mobile source exhaust emissions and alternative fuels research.

From engineering the world’s first transportable emissions testing laboratory through a steady growth that has seen it excel in the engine, chassis, field and laboratory testing, and nearly every space in the automotive spectrum, WVU CAFEE has matured from a business operating within academia into a comprehensive organization with worldclass research, test and evaluation capabilities.

CAFEE’s student researchers remain a crucial component to our success and unique story, and as graduates they continue to play leading roles in automotive research and development across the globe.
