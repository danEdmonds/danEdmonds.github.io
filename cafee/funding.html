CAFEE Funding

  • Self-supporting for 25 years
  • Over 25 years of research, over $100 million in revenues
  • $4-6 million annually in externally-funded research
  • CAFEE research appeals to both original equipment manufacturers, who must certify their equipment to meet government emissions performance standards, and government agencies, who are charged with establishing and enforcing the regulations.
